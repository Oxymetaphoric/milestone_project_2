Welcome to netBuilder

netBuilder is a card browser and deckbuilding tool for Null Signal Games' Netrunner. Netrunner is a two-player asymmetric card game set in a dystopian future where four megacorporations control almost every aspect of daily life. Legendary hackers known as “runners” aim to fight the corps' influence by hacking into their servers and preventing them from advancing their sinister agendas.
